Cache

How often remote data.jsonl and temp_info.jsonl are re-fetched for dedup.
After cache refresh or successful push, automatically remove queue entries already present in master data. Undo is offered for manual triggers; local edits on a pruned entry are lost — disable this to keep manual control.

Auto-collect Opt-in

When on, every store.steampowered.com/app/<id> page is auto-scanned and added to the queue if it's a free game. A toast in the bottom-right corner reports the outcome. Manual Add to Queue still works as before.
Same appid notifies at most once per 5 minutes per browser session to avoid spam on rapid refreshes.

Data

Back up your pending queue to a JSON file, or restore it — handy before clearing storage or moving to another browser.

Merge adds games from the file and skips any already in your queue. Replace discards your current queue first — this cannot be undone. The queue holds at most 150 games.

Danger Zone
